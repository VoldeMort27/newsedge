Risks on being Caught

The dark web poses several risks, including the possibility of being caught by law enforcement despite using encryption and other security measures. Law enforcement agencies have developed ways to track and monitor dark web activities, such as tracking IP addresses and using other forms of surveillance. Browsing through illegal content and purchasing illegal goods on the dark web can also result in law enforcement attention and potential criminal charges. It is important to be aware of these risks when using the dark web.
